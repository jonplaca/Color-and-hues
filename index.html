How it Works

Tell us about art you enjoy

Build an art profile by answering a few questions. We will personalize your own art gallery based on your individual preferences.

Every 3 months, we will ship you unique art to display in your home or business. Return older art with pre-paid shipping labels.

If you love a particular art piece, keep the art and frame! We will process payment for any unreturned art pieces.

Making art accessible to all

At Color & Hues we believe that art shouldn't be tucked away in a fancy gallery, but rather art should be present all around us. We also believe that art shouldn't be static - art should constantly be discovered, updated, and shared. As a result, we've created Color & Hues to make art more accessible, more dynamic, and more affordable for all.

In order to accomplish our goals, we've created a service where artists can display their art in common area such as offices, lobbies, libraries, and other places of everyday life. Every three months, Color & Hues will rotate new pieces of art throughout the facilities to ensure that individuals can discover new, interesting art.

We also aim to support artists by enabling the artists to expand their audience. Color & Hues works with artists to help distribute and promote the artists' latest pieces in popular areas. Furthermore, Color & Hues does not take a commission during the sale of an art piece, benefiting both buyers and sellers.

If you'd like to discover new art, remain inspired by receiving new pieces every 3 months, and supporting artists in their development, then we ask that you try Color & Hues.
